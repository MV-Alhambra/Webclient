Manual

Buy a building: Select the coins that match your desired building, then click and drag the building to your city, or to your reserve.

Take money: Select your desired coins from the market and drag them to your own coins.

Move building from city to reserve: Drag the building from your city to your reserve.

Move building from reserve to city: Drag the building from your reserve to one of the allocated spots in your city.

Swap building: Click the building which you want to swap, then drag it to the building you want to swap it with and release.

View another player's town: Click the name of the player's town you want to see on the scoreboard

Zooming in/out your town: In your city, click the minus or plus sign in the top-right corner.
